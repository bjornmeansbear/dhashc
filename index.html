Think Inside the Box™

Our methodology is based upon the logic of a cube. Design#Code provides a universal definition of services based on eight design perspectives (the corners of the cube) and the resulting twelve design arguments (the edges of the cube).

What is a Service?

Design#Code defines a service as: A Guaranteed set of outcomes and experiences fulfilled by the rendering of a customer asset (or the renting of a resource) at a particular place and time. Using this definition, the value of this service can be easily quantified with a simple business logic: Outcomes-Price/Experience=Net Value.
